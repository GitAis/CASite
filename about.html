Brian Fitzgerald Illustrator

Brianfitzer

Dublin Illustrator Brian Fitzgerald is graduate of the National College of Art and Design.

  • Winner: International Illustrated Silent Book Contest 2014
  • Winner: Creative Quarterly Summer 2014.
  • Selected for Luerzer's 200 Best Illustrations Worldwide 2014-15
  • Winner: 2014 Applied Arts Illustration Awards (Editorial)
  • Selected for Images 36, The Best of British Illustration
  • Shortlisted for Réics Carló Young Persons Book of the Year Award 2011 and 2012.
  • ICAD Bronze Award.

Brian's illustrations have been used in Advertising, Magazines, Newspapers, Annual Reports, Florist Wrapping Paper, Greeting Cards, Cereal boxes, Children’s Picture books.

Clients include:

  • Editorial

    • The Boston Globe, The Wall Street Journal, The Irish Times, Hotelier Magazine Canada.

  • Illustration for Design

    • Aer Lingus, Tesco, Safeway, Courvoisier, Guinness Hopstore, Hallmark Cards, Brown Thomas, Bank of Ireland, AIB Bank, Irish Permanent, Educational Building Society, Fáilte Ireland, Dublin Tourism, Baker Security and Networks, Tullis Russell, Priority Management, Krokante Museli Germany, Centra, Robert Roberts Coffee, ILTP Consulting, Birr Castle Demesne, Combat Poverty Ireland, Pavilion Theatre, Beawleys, Abrakebabra, Sony Music, ANDEC Communications, Dublin Docklands, Aboretum Garden Centre, Futa Fata Publishing.
